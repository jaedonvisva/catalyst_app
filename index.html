About Me!

Technology has always been my passion in life. I am known amongst my friends, family and teachers to be the resident tech expert who is remarkable at solving technical problems. Computers have intrigued me since I was as young as 5 years old, and I started to learn how to code when I was 7. Since then, I have learned multiple programming languages, such as Python, and web design languages such as HTML and CSS, and JavaScript. My parents have always taught me to think big and that to be an achiever, I must be a believer. They encourage me to not just be a user of technology, but to be an innovator. I am inspired by my favourite show Shark Tank, and my dream is to pitch my own technological invention on that show one day, one that provides a solution to a problem that is currently not solvable by human intelligence. I look to create solutions to the security threat that we face today, and use conputer vision and machine learning to do so. When I look at how much technology, especially artificial intelligence, is influencing our lives in the current digital world of self-driving cars and robots performing surgeries, I often wonder what the world would be like in the next 10-15 years. I want to be a valuable innovative contributor in that world. Being part of the Catalyst would also provide me with a great opportunity to be with students who are ambitious, have similar interests, goals, hopes and dreams and who see the world through the same technological lens as I do. I strongly believe that the Catalyst program would prove to be a resourceful and nurturing leadership learning environment for me and will be a big stepping stone in making my dream of being in Shark Tank, come true one day. I am excited to be part of the next generation of thinkers and innovators.
